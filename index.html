Non-preemptive · Processes execute in arrival order · Simple but may cause convoy effect
Shortest Job First
Non-preemptive · Picks the process with shortest burst time — minimizes average waiting time
Round Robin
Preemptive · Each process gets a fixed time quantum — ensures fair CPU allocation
Priority Scheduling
Non-preemptive · Lower priority number = higher priority — may cause starvation
Mutex — Mutual Exclusion
